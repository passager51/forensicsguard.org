FORENSICSGUARD

Forensic intelligence platform

ForensicsGuard is a forensic intelligence platform focused on advanced backdoors and state-sponsored spyware, including threats such as Pegasus, Predator, and FinFisher. It is designed for investigators, forensic labs, journalists, and security-sensitive organizations that require evidence-driven analysis. The platform combines an Android mobile sensor APK, an optional hardware intercept appliance, a Lab Station, and an AI-assisted forensic engine. IOC correlation uses public research and threat intelligence sources such as Amnesty International Security Lab, Citizen Lab, CISA KEV, and ForensicsGuard Intelligence. The Android APK operates under mobile OS restrictions. It can identify behavioral indicators, suspicious network activity, risky permissions, and IOC matches that may reveal traces linked to advanced spyware activity. It does not guarantee full detection of highly evasive spyware. There is no silver bullet. ForensicsGuard delivers its full value when combined with human investigation and the complete ecosystem, enabling deeper analysis, correlation, and evidence validation.
